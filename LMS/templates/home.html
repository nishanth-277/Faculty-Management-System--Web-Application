VFA translates to "VIT Faculty Availability System"

The VIT Faculty Availability System streamlines student access to faculty availability in their cabins, enhancing communication and fostering academic support. This user-friendly platform ensures students can efficiently locate and engage with faculty members, promoting collaboration and academic success within the VIT community.
